Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Bookings and Cancellations +

How do I make a booking?

  • From the 8-day date list, click on the date you want to book.
  • Click on the session block for the time you want.
  • If the booking is successful, your name will appear in the session block.

How do I cancel a booking?

  • Click on your name in the session block you want to cancel.
  • If the cancellation is successful, your name will be removed from the session block.
  • Use the View Bookings link to display all your current weekly bookings and cancel them from there.
  • In the View Bookings link, the "Cancel" button will display "Cannot Cancel" for expired bookings

What are the booking rules?

  • You can only book sessions for the next 8 days.
  • You cannot make a booking if your lights credit is below 0.00 NAD.
  • You cannot book a session that has already started.
  • Each week (Monday to Sunday), weekly booking limits are reset.
  • A message will appear when the daily or weekly booking limit is reached, displaying the limit details.

What are the cancellation rules?

  • You cannot cancel bookings that have already started.
  • You cannot cancel bookings made by other players.
  • You cannot cancel bookings that are in the past. Court time slots will show as Unavailable.

What is the cost of online booking or cancelling?

  • Booking costs can be changed at any time.
  • Booking costs N$1.00.
  • Cancellation costs N$0.50.
Legend +

What is the purpose of the Time Slot Legend?

  • The Time Slot Legend helps you understand the session periods available for booking. Each period is colour-coded to represent specific booking categories, such as Normal or Peak Time.
  • Next to each Time Slot Legend description is the cost for the full 45-minute court session.

What is Waiting List Availability?

  • Waiting List Availability shows the availability of sessions that are full. You can join the waiting list for these sessions if they become available later.
  • Click on the coloured Time box to be added to the waiting list. Click again to be removed. Make sure your email address is correct in the My Profile section.

Where is the Lights Credit value displayed?

  • Your current Lights Credit is displayed in the legend section, so you can easily check your balance while booking.
System Information +

How long can I use the system?

  • You can use the system for as long as you want, but the system will log you out after 5 minutes of inactivity.
  • For security reasons, it is recommended to log out as soon as you finish making or cancelling your bookings.
Additional Tips for Players +

What is Peak Time?

  • Peak Time sessions are the busiest and are marked in the specific colour as shown on the Time Slot Legend.
  • Make sure to plan ahead as slots may fill up quickly.

What happens if I have insufficient credit?

  • If your lights credit falls below 0.00 NAD, you will not be able to make new bookings.
  • Check your credit regularly and top up if needed to avoid missing out.
  • Contact the squash committee if unsure about top-up options.

What if I cannot make a session I have booked?

  • If bookings are not cancelled, a penalty fee may be applied depending on the Time Slot Period.

What should I do if the system logs me out?

  • Simply log back in to continue using the system.
  • Avoid extended periods of inactivity to prevent being logged out.

What should I do if the system is not loading

  • Simply logout and login again.
  • Refresh the webpage.
